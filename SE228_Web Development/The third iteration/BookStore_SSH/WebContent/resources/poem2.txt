South African born Elon Musk is the renowned entrepreneur and innovator behind PayPal, 
SpaceX, Tesla, and SolarCity. Musk wants to save our planet; he wants to send citizens 
into space, to form a colony on Mars; he wants to make money while doing these things; 
and he wants us all to know about it. He is the real-life inspiration for the 
Iron Man series of films starring Robert Downey Junior.
